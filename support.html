Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel an order?

You can cancel within 5 minutes of placing your order, before the chef begins preparation. Go to Orders → your order → Cancel. After preparation has started, cancellation is no longer possible.

My order was wrong or had quality issues — what do I do?

Contact us at hello@duemo.ai within 2 hours of receiving your order. Include your order number and a description of the issue. We'll review and process a refund if warranted.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account in the app via Profile → Settings → Delete Account, or by emailing privacy@duemo.az. See our Account Deletion page for full details.

I have a food allergy — how do I know if a dish is safe?

Each dish listing includes an allergen section. You can also contact the chef directly through the app before ordering. DUEMO does not guarantee allergen-free preparation environments.

How do I request my personal data?

Email privacy@duemo.az from your registered email address with the subject "Data Access Request." We will respond within 30 days.

I want to become a chef on DUEMO — how do I apply?

Email hello@duemo.ai with the subject "Chef Application" and tell us about yourself and your cooking. We'll get back to you with the next steps.

Часто задаваемые вопросы

Как отменить заказ?

Отменить можно в течение 5 минут после оформления, до начала приготовления. Заказы → ваш заказ → Отменить.

Как удалить аккаунт?

В приложении: Профиль → Настройки → Удалить аккаунт. Или напишите на privacy@duemo.az. Подробности: страница удаления аккаунта.

Как запросить свои персональные данные?

Отправьте письмо на privacy@duemo.az с темой "Запрос данных" с вашего зарегистрированного адреса. Ответим в течение 30 дней.
