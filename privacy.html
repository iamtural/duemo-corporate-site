Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Effective: October 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how "DUEMO" MMC ("DUEMO", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and website.

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • Name and contact details (email, phone number)

  • Order history and preferences
  • Device information and app usage data

We do not collect unnecessary personal data.

2. Payment & Card Data

🔐 DUEMO does not store bank card details on its servers. All payment transactions are processed through certified payment processors using TLS 1.2 encryption. Card data is transmitted securely and never retained by DUEMO.

3. Security Protocol

Our platform uses TLS 1.2 encryption for all data transmitted between users and our servers. The platform operates on a static IP address.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Processing and delivering your orders

  • Improving the platform and user experience
  • Legal compliance and fraud prevention
  • Sending order updates and service notifications

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
