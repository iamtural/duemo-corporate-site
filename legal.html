This Privacy Policy describes how "DUEMO" MMC ("Duemo", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use the Duemo mobile applications (Customer, Chef, Courier) and website. By using our services, you agree to the practices described here.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process orders, payments, and deliveries
  • To connect customers with chefs and couriers
  • To send push notifications about your orders
  • To verify identity and prevent fraud
  • To improve our platform, safety standards, and user experience
  • To comply with Azerbaijan food safety (AFSA) and tax regulations
  • To respond to your support requests

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as necessary to operate the service:

  • Chefs see the customer's name, order details, and delivery zone (not full address)
  • Couriers see the pick-up address (chef location) and delivery address
  • Payment processors (certified third parties) handle card transactions — Duemo does not store full card numbers
  • Identity verification providers may process government ID for chef and courier onboarding
  • Legal authorities if required by the laws of the Republic of Azerbaijan

4. Payment & Card Data

💳 Duemo does not store full card numbers, CVV codes, or banking credentials on its servers. All payment processing is handled by certified third-party payment processors. We store only the card type and last 4 digits for your reference.

5. Location Data

Customer App: Location access is optional and used only to suggest nearby chefs and to share your approximate location with the courier during active delivery. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

Courier App: Couriers must enable precise GPS location while an active delivery is in progress. This data is used to coordinate the delivery and display estimated arrival time to the customer. Location is not tracked outside of active delivery sessions.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — update inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data (see Account Deletion)
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@duemo.az. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Push Notifications

We send push notifications about order status, delivery updates, and promotions. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings. This will not affect your ability to use the app or view order status within the app.

9. Cookies & Device Identifiers

The Duemo website uses functional cookies necessary for session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Our mobile apps use device identifiers (such as FCM tokens) solely to deliver push notifications. These are not shared with advertising networks.

10. Children's Privacy

Duemo is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at privacy@duemo.az for immediate removal.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification or email. Continued use of Duemo after the effective date of changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Duemo is a marketplace platform connecting home chefs, couriers, and customers for home-cooked food delivery in Baku, Azerbaijan. All food is prepared by registered independent home chefs who comply with Azerbaijan Food Safety Agency (AFSA) requirements.

2. Account Registration

All chefs on Duemo operate in compliance with the applicable food safety regulations of the Republic of Azerbaijan and AFSA requirements. 9. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time via the app (Profile → Settings → Delete Account) or by emailing privacy@duemo.az. See the Account Deletion section below for details on data handling.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel an order?
Orders can be cancelled within 5 minutes of placement, before the chef begins preparation. Go to Profile → Orders → [Your Order] → Cancel Order.

How do I report a quality issue?
Contact us at hello@duemo.ai within 2 hours of receiving your delivery. Include your order number and a description of the issue. Photos are helpful.

How do I delete my account?
Go to Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account. Or email privacy@duemo.az. Full details in the Account Deletion section above.

I have a food allergy. What should I do?
Contact the chef directly through the app's in-app messaging before placing your order. Duemo chefs are required to disclose allergen information upon request.

How do I request a copy of my personal data?
Email privacy@duemo.az with the subject "Data Access Request." We will respond within 30 days.

How do I apply to cook or deliver for Duemo?
Email chefs@duemo.ai (for chefs) or couriers@duemo.ai (for couriers). Our team will guide you through the onboarding and AFSA compliance process.
